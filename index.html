My name is Wiley Brouillette, I am a designer holding a Bachelor of Architecture.

I don't believe project barriers are meant to be climbed, but I have an undeniable instinct to climb even if barriers can be easily evaded. This has unintentionally become an ethos shaping the skills and projects I develop, and the reason for their diversity. There's no all-encompassing focus across my work, instead each project has its own focus to which I can only respond, "evading be damned!" I've practiced digital & print production, architecture, and game design. It would be inaccurate to say I'm an architect, illustrator, game designer, or programmer. Instead it's easier to say I will not stop climbing.
